Rapid and Remote Deauthentication Drone (R2D2)

Welcome to R2D2, a tool used to perform a remote WiFi deauthentication attack against an enemy network to render it useless. Use the buttons below to scan for networks, launch an attack, or view previous scan logs.

Use this button to launch a scan for WiFi networks and their associated devices in the area.

Use this button to launch an attack on a detected WiFi network that was located during a scan.

Use this button to display the logs of previous network scans.

We are using Node.js , Chromium , and Electron .
